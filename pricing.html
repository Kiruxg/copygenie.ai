Frequently Asked Questions

What's included in the free plan?

+

The free plan includes 5 AI-generated descriptions per month, basic SEO optimization, and English-only support. Perfect for trying out CopyGenie.

Can I upgrade or downgrade anytime?

+

Yes! You can change your plan at any time. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What languages do you support?

+

Pro plan supports 5 major languages, while Business plan supports 10+ languages including regional variations.

Do unused descriptions roll over?

+

No, descriptions reset at the start of each month. However, Business plan users get unlimited descriptions.
